# Parmenides

Argued that change and plurality are illusion; what *is* is one, eternal, unchanging Being.

## Disagreed with
- [[Heraclitus]] :: Is change real? — change is mere appearance

## Cruxes
- [[Is change real?]] — no; Being is one and motionless
