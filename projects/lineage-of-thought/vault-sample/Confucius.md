# Confucius

Kong Fuzi. Cultivate virtue through ritual (*li*), learning, and filial relationships; lead by moral example.

## Cruxes
- [[Is human nature good?]] — perfectible through ritual and learning
- [[How to order society?]] — graded relationships, ritual, the rectification of names
