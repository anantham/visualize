# Zeno of Citium

Founded Stoicism in the Athenian Stoa. Live according to nature and reason; virtue is the only good.

## Influenced by
- [[Socrates]] — via the Cynics

## Disagreed with
- [[Epicurus]] :: What is the good life? — virtue, not pleasure

## Cruxes
- [[What is the good life?]] — live according to reason; virtue suffices
