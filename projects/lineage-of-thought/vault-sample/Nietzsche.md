# Nietzsche

Philosopher with a hammer. Becoming over being; revalue all values; beyond good and evil.

## Revives
- [[Heraclitus]] — flux and becoming, ~2300 years on; eternal recurrence

## Disagreed with
- [[Socrates]] :: How should one live? — rejects reason-over-instinct; 'Socrates the decadent'
- [[Plato]] :: Are universals real? — there is no 'true world' of Forms behind this one

## Cruxes
- [[Is change real?]] — yes; becoming is all there is
- [[How should one live?]] — beyond good and evil; affirm life
