# Adi Shankara

Consolidated Advaita (non-dual) Vedanta. The world is *maya*; only Brahman is real, and atman = Brahman.

## Disagreed with
- [[Nagarjuna]] :: Is emptiness the final truth? — no; the one reality is Brahman, not the void

## Cruxes
- [[Is the self real?]] — yes; atman is Brahman (against the Buddha)
