# Zhu Xi

Synthesized Neo-Confucianism; defined the canon (the Four Books) that ruled exams for centuries.

## Revives
- [[Confucius]] — reanimates the classical tradition ~1600 years on
- [[Mencius]] — canonizes the Mencian line of human goodness

## Influenced by
- [[Buddha]] — absorbs and counters Buddhist metaphysics that crossed from India (?)
