# Plato

Founded the Academy. Cast the sensible world as shadows of eternal Forms.

## Influenced by
- [[Socrates]] — his teacher and the voice of the dialogues
- [[Parmenides]] — unchanging Being became the eternal Forms
- [[Heraclitus]] — flux, but only of the *sensible* world (via Cratylus)

## Cruxes
- [[Are universals real?]] — yes; the Forms exist apart from particulars
