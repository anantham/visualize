# Xunzi

The hard-edged Confucian. Human nature is bad; goodness is a deliberate human achievement.

## Develops
- [[Confucius]] — ritual as the engineering of virtue

## Disagreed with
- [[Mencius]] :: Is human nature good? — no; nature is bad, goodness is acquired

## Cruxes
- [[Is human nature good?]] — no; it must be reshaped by ritual and teaching
