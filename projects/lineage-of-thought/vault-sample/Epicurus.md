# Epicurus

Founded the Garden. The good life is tranquil pleasure (*ataraxia*): absence of pain and fear.

## Disagreed with
- [[Zeno of Citium]] :: What is the good life? — measured pleasure, not bare virtue

## Cruxes
- [[What is the good life?]] — freedom from disturbance (ataraxia)
