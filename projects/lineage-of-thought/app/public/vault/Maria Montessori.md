# Maria Montessori

Italian physician and educator who developed a scientific, child-centered pedagogy based on observation of children’s spontaneous activity; her method, emphasizing prepared environments, mixed-age classrooms, and hands-on materials, spread globally and influenced early childhood education, special education, and peace education.

## Cruxes
- [[What is the nature of the child’s mind?]] — absorbent mind; children have sensitive periods for language, order, sensory refinement, and social development
- [[How should education foster peace?]] — through normalization, concentration, and respect for the child’s dignity; “education for peace” begins in the prepared environment

## Idea travel
- 1907 — Rome: opens first Casa dei Bambini in San Lorenzo slum
- 1939 — 1947 — India: interned during WWII; trains thousands of teachers and refines cosmic education
