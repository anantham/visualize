# Rabindranath Tagore

Bengali polymath, poet, composer, and educator who founded Visva-Bharati University at Santiniketan; first non-European Nobel laureate in Literature (1913), he reshaped modern Indian literature, music, and art while advocating a universal humanism that transcended colonial and nationalist boundaries.

## Influenced by
- [[Kabir]] — mystic poetry, syncretic spirituality, and critique of ritualism

## Cruxes
- [[What is the role of art and creativity in human life?]] — essential path to realizing the infinite within the finite
- [[Is nationalism compatible with universal humanity?]] — no; it must be transcended by a broader cosmopolitan spirit
