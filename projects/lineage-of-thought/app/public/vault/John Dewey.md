# John Dewey

American pragmatist philosopher, psychologist, and educational reformer whose laboratory school at the University of Chicago and writings on democracy, experience, and inquiry shaped progressive education worldwide; he viewed philosophy as a tool for improving democratic life and resolving practical problems.

## Influenced by
- [[Charles Sanders Peirce]] — pragmatist theory of truth and inquiry

## Cruxes
- [[What is the nature of knowledge and inquiry?]] — instrumental; ideas are tools tested by their consequences in experience
- [[What is the relationship between education and democracy?]] — education is essential to democracy as a way of associated living; schools should be embryonic communities

- 1896 — Chicago: founds Laboratory School implementing progressive methods
- 1916 — USA: publishes Democracy and Education
