# Jiddu Krishnamurti

Philosopher and speaker who dissolved the Order of the Star in 1929, rejecting the messianic role assigned to him by the Theosophical Society; he spent decades in public dialogues worldwide emphasizing direct perception, freedom from psychological conditioning, and the indivisibility of the observer and the observed.

## Cruxes
- [[Is there a path to truth?]] — no; truth is pathless and must be discovered anew each moment through choiceless awareness
- [[Can psychological conditioning be ended?]] — yes; through direct insight into the movement of thought and the self

## Idea travel
- 1929 — Ojai, California: dissolves Order of the Star, declares “Truth is a pathless land”
- 1986 — Worldwide: foundations continue disseminating talks and writings in multiple languages
